# Doctrine Stability Declaration

The Applicability Boundary Doctrine is published
as a conceptual reference framework.

Version v1.x establishes the canonical terminology,
core definition, and structural properties of
applicability boundaries.

Future work may extend the doctrine with:

– case studies
– domain-specific interpretations
– academic discussion

However, the conceptual core defined in this version
is considered stable.

The doctrine describes structural limits of operational
validity but does not prescribe engineering
implementations or control mechanisms.
